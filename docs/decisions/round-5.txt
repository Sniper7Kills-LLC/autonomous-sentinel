Q158: Web-canonical audio format — derived from original at upload, served to browser. Pick exact codec + bitrate:
  (a) MP3 96 kbps mono — universal browser support, ~700 KB per 60s; "safe" choice
  (b) MP3 64 kbps mono — adequate for voice, ~500 KB per 60s
  (c) Opus 32 kbps mono — best quality-per-byte for voice (~250 KB per 60s), all modern browsers, no IE/old-Safari
  (d) AAC 64 kbps mono — Apple-friendly, ~500 KB per 60s
  Recommend (c) Opus — voice-optimized, smallest file. EAM listeners are technical and on modern browsers.
